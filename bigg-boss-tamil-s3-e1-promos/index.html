Bigg Boss Tamil S3 E1 Promos

Gokul June 24, 2019 Bigg Boss

Promo 1 -

Contestants wake up energetic on their first day, every one looks happy to be in Bigg Boss house. When the promo ends it looks like sandy fell inside the empty pool.

Promo 2 -

Contestants get a welcome message from Bigg Boss when they are assembled in the lounge area. Bigg Boss explains that this season there are metered limits for some essentials like cooking gas & water. Contestants clap for that & Fathima stands up & says this is not a matter of joy but rather the sorry state of affairs that our people have to face this issue. Cheran explainst that they clapped to thank Bigg Boss for taking such measures to save water.

Promo 3 -

Sandy says that he wants to learn the art of singing from Mohan Vaithya & sings in his own way, after a while Mohan Vaithya seems disturbed by sandy's singing talents.
